The Basics:


This works by processing images and giving them a "nudity score" from -100 to 100 (if everything works as it should).

When the score goes over a Threshold, it will activate the setting, all thresholds can be adjusted in the settings.

If you want to manually confirm that someone isn't *in the nude* you can click on their video or the button to say so, this will make them not be skipped

If someone is going to be automatically skipped, there will be a bar above their video which goes down to indicate when they are going to be skipped
